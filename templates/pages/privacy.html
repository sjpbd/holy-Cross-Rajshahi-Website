1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and student details when you interact with our website, fill out forms, or contact us. This includes:

  • Name and Contact Details
  • Student Identification Info
  • Academic Performance Data
  • Inquiry and Feedback Forms

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to enhance the educational experience and maintain school operations:

Educational Services

To provide support and maintain academic records.

Communication

To keep parents and students informed via SMS or email.

Admissions

To process applications and handle enrollment inquiries.

Service Improvement

To analyze website traffic and improve user experience.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our servers are secured and access is limited to authorized personnel only.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our trusted educational partners and government bodies as required by law.

5. Changes to This Policy

Holy Cross School and College has the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time. We encourage users to frequently check this page for any changes. Your continued use of the website following the posting of changes will be deemed your acceptance of those changes.
